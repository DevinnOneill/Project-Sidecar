# SideCar Command Profile: Strategic Vision & Development Plan

> **Domain:** Module-Specific Development Roadmap
> **Module:** MOD-CMD (`app/command.html`)
> **Date:** March 2026

---

## 1. Core Purpose

The `command.html` module is the macro-level operational surface for the **Force Architect** (Placement Officers and Rating Evaluators). While the workspace and Sailor modules focus on individual career coaching, the Command page zooms out to assess the collective health, combat readiness, and billet execution of a specific Navy command.

Its strategic mandate is to fuse raw manning metrics (O.BA/E.BA vs. actuals) with predictive pipeline analytics, enabling proactive talent distribution rather than reactive gap-filling constraint management.

## 2. Interface Philosophy (The Force Architect View)

The Command UI must adhere to the Covenant Design System (white surfaces, brass/khaki gold accents, DM Mono for data). It operates as a strategic dashboard, stripping away individual noise to highlight structural vulnerabilities. 

A Force Architect does not log in to merely see "who is there." They need to instantly understand:
- Is this command operationally healthy today?
- What critical gaps are opening in the next 3, 6, and 12 months?
- Who is inbound to relieve those gaps, and where are the pipeline risks?

## 3. Structural Components of the Record

To empower the Force Architect, the UI is organized into four distinct architectural zones:

### A. The Command Health Diagnostic
The top-level summary of the command's operational readiness.
- **Manning Percentages:** Immediate presentation of current vs. authorized billets (e.g., "92% Fit / 88% Fill").
- **Triage Alerts:** High-priority structural warnings generated by the system (e.g., "Critical shortage in 1110 DESIG" or "Lead NEC absent").
- **Operational Status:** Command type, MCA, TYCOM, and current operational phase.

### B. Billet Pipeline & Gap Forecasting
A predictive visual model of the command's talent pipeline over time.
- **Horizon Mapping:** A timeline view showing current onboard talent against Projected Rotation Dates (PRDs) and Estimated Loss Dates (ELDs).
- **Critical Vacancies:** A filtered queue of billets that are currently gapped or projected to be gapped within a critical window, sorted by operational impact.
- **Inbound Tracking:** Visibility on Sailors under orders to the command, their projected arrival dates, and their specific qualification impact on the command's readiness.

### C. The Authoritative Core (Billet Roster)
A highly dense, rigorously styled roster of all billets within the command, replacing the legacy Activity Manning Status Listing (AMSL).
- **Billet Sequence Codes (BSC):** Rendered strictly in `--font-data` DM Mono.
- **Incumbent Mapping:** Shows the Sailor filling the billet, their PRD, and alignment accuracy (Paygrade, Rate, NEC match).
- **Flagged Misalignments:** Instant visual indicators when a Sailor is incorrectly mapped to a billet or lacks the required AQD/NEC.

### D. The Distribution Action Engine
- **1-Click Communication:** Pathways to instantly contact the Command Career Counselor (CCC) or command triad regarding manning issues.
- **Requisition Prioritization:** Interface for Placement Officers to elevate specific billet gaps based on emerging operational needs, feeding directly into the matching algorithm.

## 4. Development Requirements

### Adherence to C-Constraints
- **C-14 (PRD Semantic Lock):** When displaying inbound/outbound timelines, PRD urgency colors must strictly match the 5-tier semantic system.
- **C-08 (Module Boundary):** The `command.html` page must remain self-contained. All data loads for the command profile (billets, aggregated sailor data) must route through `SideCarAdapter.getCommands()` and `getBillets()` within `app.js`.
- **C-09 / C-03 (Data Security):** Given the macro nature of command rollups, no aggregate data can cross PII/CUI boundaries during Phase 1A. Synthetic command structures (fabricated UICs, unit names, and rosters) must be used exclusively.

## 5. Conclusion

The `command.html` page is the strategic counterpart to the individual Sailor view. By synthesizing incoming talent pipelines with real-time gap forecasting, SideCar transforms Placement Officers from reactive clerks hunting for bodies to proactive architects shaping fleet readiness long before a deployment begins.
